## Current Progress (Research Phase)
I am currently completing:
- Geometry and LED path planning  
- Power system calculations  
- Frame design research  
- Budget feasibility  
- Material selection  


---

## Planned Hardware
- WS2812B LED strip (5m, 144LED/m)
- 2×3 ft acrylic sheet
- One‑way mirror film
- 5V 30A power supply
- ESP32 microcontroller
- PLA filament for 3D printing
- Single‑core + stranded wire
- Heat shrink + connectors

---

## Goals
- Build a fully functional infinity dodecahedron  
- Write custom LED animations  
- Document the entire process for Macondo  
- Keep the total cost under the $100 Tier 1 limit
